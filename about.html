Our Story

We founded Café Grumpy in 2005 with the dream of serving great tasting specialty coffee to everyone in a welcoming environment. Since our first café in Greenpoint to now eleven thriving locations, we’ve stayed hands-on and committed to excellence.

Since 2009, we’ve roasted our coffee in-house and built lasting partnerships with our producing partners at origin. Certified as a Women-Owned Business Enterprise, we strive to focus on community, sustainability, and social responsibility.

With the dedication of our baristas, team members, and support from customers like you, we continue to grow. Our goal is simple: if you come in grumpy, you leave happy!
